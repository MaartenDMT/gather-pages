Gather Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DMT Entertainments handles information in Gather, a party game app for local and optional online play.

Data We Collect

Gather does not require users to create an account. The app may process player nicknames, room codes, room settings, gameplay state, app interactions, purchase status, subscription status, AI credit usage, and app-generated identifiers.

Local app settings, local player lists, consent state, and game preferences may be stored on the user's device.

Online Rooms

If users choose to use online rooms, player nicknames, room codes, room settings, and game state may be sent to Gather's server so participants can join and play together. Users can use nicknames instead of real names.

Purchases and Subscriptions

Gather may offer optional purchases, subscriptions, or AI prompt credits. Purchases are processed through Google Play Billing and RevenueCat. Gather does not receive full payment card details. RevenueCat and Google Play may process transaction records, purchase status, subscription status, and app-generated customer identifiers to provide paid features and prevent fraud.

AI Prompt Generation

If users choose to use AI prompt generation, Gather may send game settings, selected options, and prompt-generation requests to Gather's server and an AI provider. Users should not enter sensitive personal information into AI prompt requests.

How We Use Data

Data is used to provide app functionality, including local gameplay, optional online rooms, paid feature access, subscription checks, credit balances, AI prompt generation, security, fraud prevention, and compliance.

Sharing

Gather may share limited data with service providers needed to run the app, including hosting providers, RevenueCat, Google Play Billing, and AI providers. Gather does not sell user data.

Security

Data sent between the app, Gather's server, and third-party services is transmitted using encrypted connections where supported. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but Gather uses reasonable safeguards for the data it processes.

Retention and Deletion

Local app data can be deleted from the app settings or by uninstalling the app. Server-side room data is retained only as needed to provide online room functionality and maintenance. Purchase and subscription records may be retained by Google Play, RevenueCat, or DMT Entertainments as required for billing, fraud prevention, tax, legal, platform compliance, or support.

Users can request deletion of server-side Gather data by following the instructions on the Data Deletion Requests page.

Children

Gather is not designed for children under 13. Optional After Party content is behind an 18+ confirmation.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes or when legal, platform, or service requirements change. The updated policy will be posted on this page.

Contact
