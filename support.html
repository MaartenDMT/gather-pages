Gather Support and Data Deletion

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Gather is a party game app by DMT Entertainments. For app issues, general feedback, feature requests, privacy questions, custom prompt moderation concerns, prompt report follow-up, or data deletion requests, contact dmt2014.business@gmail.com.

Local data reset

In the app, open Settings and choose Reset Local Data to clear local players, sessions, generated prompts, custom prompts, consent, and saved room data from the current device.

Account and server data deletion

Gather is local-first and does not require an account for core play. If online rooms, AI credits, or other server-backed features are enabled, request deletion by emailing the support contact above with the subject "Gather account deletion request". Include the room code, player nickname, or app-generated account identifier if available so the developer can locate server records.

Account deletion means deletion of the entire account record and associated personal data that Gather is not legally required to keep. Temporary deactivation or disabling is not treated as account deletion.

If deletion is manual, the developer will explain how long the request is expected to take and send confirmation when deletion is completed.

If service providers process account data for enabled features, the developer will delete records from Gather systems and request deletion from those service providers where applicable. Some records may be retained when needed for security, fraud prevention, payment reconciliation, tax, refund, or legal compliance.

Custom prompt reports

Shared custom prompts are reviewed before publication. In online games, use Report Prompt to flag approved shared prompts and Block Source to stop prompts from the same source on the current device. You can also email the support contact above for moderation concerns.

Read the Gather Privacy Policy or the focused Data Deletion Requests page.
